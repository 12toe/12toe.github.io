THE POCKET BOOK

A real pocketable notebook, smartphone sized.

The Pocket Book is a product that started development in the early 2021s. As an avid user of sketchbooks on the regular, I was no stranger to books of various sizes. However, the one thing that irked me the most was the so-called "pocket" sized notebooks. Books under this label are either: 3.5"x5.5", 4.1"x5.8" (A6), 4.9"x6.9" (B6). Limited to the standard sizes. And any book that was actually 3"x5", they opened on the short-end like a reporter's notebook.

I wanted something that I could take with me as often as I carry my phone. Yet nothing out there that was pre-existing was what I was looking for -a notebook that was slim enough to fit in my pocket. If our phones have adapted to the pocket, why haven't our notebooks? And so I decided to take matters into my own hands -pockets for that matter.
