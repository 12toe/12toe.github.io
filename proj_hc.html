HYPERCROSS

Antigravity racing at blindingly fast speeds. Are you in?

HyperCross is a personal project of mine that I've been developing for some time now. Inspired by games such as F-Zero GX, Star Wars Episode 1: Racer, and XG2 --I wanted to create my own interpretation of the genre. It has gone through many iterations as I wade my way through learning the solo game-development process, but each time I see it getting more and more refined as time goes on.
