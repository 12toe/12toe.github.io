ABOUT ME

Hello

Hailing from Michigan, I emerged from a vibrant artistic community of family and friends, who helped foster a creative environment from childhood to obtaining an Illustration degree at College for Creative Studies in Detroit. Expanding my horizons, I delved into concept design, furthering my studies at Brainstorm, CGMA, and Gnomon in the Los Angeles, California area.

Beyond the art scene, I enjoy reading, game development, and playing board and video games, especially Dance Dance Revolution (favorite genres: racing, rhythm, puzzle, metroidvania, adventure). Currently residing in LA working as a freelance concept artist and illustrator, I continue to explore how art and design connect with people's perceptions of the world around them, all while occasionally dancing on some arrow panels. ← ↓ ↑ →

Previous Clients: Naughty Dog, Respawn Entertainment, Electronic Arts, Industrial Toys, TiMi Studio Group, Tencent Games, Midnight Society
